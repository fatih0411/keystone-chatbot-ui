Keystone Chatbot Embed Demo

How to Embed the Chatbot

To add the chatbot to your website, simply add the following script tag to your HTML:

<script src="https://fatih0411.github.io/keystone-chatbot-ui/embed.js"></script>

Demo Content

This is a demo page showing how the chatbot appears when embedded. The chatbot should appear as a floating window in the bottom-right corner of the page.
